Welcome to Neocity

A cybertech-inspired multiverse social platform where users exist as customizable digital beings. Explore character designs, read about the project's vision, and track our progress.

Project Vision & Goals

Our primary goal is to create an engaging and user-friendly experience that achieves the following:

  • Deep Customization: Deliver a rich, modular system for character and personal space customization.
  • Social Connection: Foster a vibrant community through robust social features and shared spaces.
  • Immersive Aesthetic: Build a visually stunning cybertech world with a unique, moody atmosphere.

The World

Premise

In the distant future, humanity has created Neocity, a digital multiverse where consciousness can be uploaded. The central hub is "The Grid," a vast, ever-expanding city characterized by a perpetual twilight, rain-slicked streets, and a constant shimmer of floating data particles.

Visual Theme

  • Color Palette: Dominated by dark themes, with navy blue, deep grays, and black as the base.
  • Accent Colors: Vibrant, glowing neon purple, blue, and magenta for UI elements and effects.
  • Atmosphere: Moody and immersive with floating particle effects like data motes and digital rain.
